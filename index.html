Project 3

A growing support for lowering the MLDA to 18 has re-emerged in recent years, especially amongst college students. People petitioning to lower the minimum drinking age hypothesizes the ineffectiveness of MLDA at decreasing alcohol consumption. Using difference-in-difference model in conjunction with fixed-effect model, I was able to analyze the effectiveness of 21-MLDA at preventing alcohol consumption and drunk driving.
